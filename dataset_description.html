Paired soil and rice grain samples were collected from the rice growing arsenic contaminated sites of West Bengal, India covering the districts of Nadia, Murshidabad, North 24 Parganas, Bardhaman, Maldah, Hooghly under both rainfed and irrigated rice systems. The soil paraments viz. pH, organic carbon, available phosphorus, available Iron, plant available arsenic and arsenic in rice grain samples were analysed as per the protocols outlined in Mandal et al., (2023).
